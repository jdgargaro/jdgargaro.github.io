About Us

Gargaro's Bakery first fired up the ovens in September of 1986, we are proud to be apart of the Arvada community, as well as family-owned and operated, for nearly 30 years! Danny and Nancy Gargaro are the proud owners/proprietors of Gargaro'ss, and with the help of their six children, have made Gargaro's into one of Arvada's best kept secrets.
